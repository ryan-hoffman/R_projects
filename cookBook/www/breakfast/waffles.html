Waffles

2 c flour

2 t baking soda

1 t baking powder

1 t salt

6 T butter, cold

1 1/4 c milk

3 eggs, separated


  1. Preheat waffle iron to 350 degrees.
  2. Melt the butter in a small saucepan and set aside.
  3. Combine the dry ingredients in a large mixing bowl.
  4. Separate the eggs. Set the yolks aside and in another bowl, beat the egg whites to the soft peak stage.
  5. Combine the butter, egg yolks and milk, and add the mixture to the dry ingredients.
  6. Mix until all of the ingredients come together. Gently fold in the egg whites.
  7. Spoon into the preheated waffle iron.
  8. Cook until golden brown.
  9. Serve immediately!
