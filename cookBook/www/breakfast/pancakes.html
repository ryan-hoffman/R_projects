Pancakes

1 1/2 c flour

1 1/2 t baking soda

1/2 t baking powder

1 t salt

2 T butter, melted

1 c buttermilk, whey or milk

2 eggs


Note: if using milk, add 1 t of lemon juice


  1. Preheat griddle to 350 degrees.
  2. Combine the dry ingredients in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Combine wet ingredients in another bowl and pour into the dry.
  4. Mix just until the ingredients come together.
  5. Ladle the desired pancake size onto the hot griddle.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the top and the bottom is golden brown.
  7. Flip and cook on the other side until done. Flip only once.

Pancakes are one of Stella's favorite breakfasts! We freeze the extras and heat 'em up when we want a quick breakfast or an afternoon snack.
